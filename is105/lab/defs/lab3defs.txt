Regular expression:
Regular expression is in theoretical computer science, a sequence of characters that forms a search pattern, mainly for use in pattern matching with strings, or string matching. Each character in a regular expression is either understood to be a metacharacter with a special meaning, or a regular character with its literal meaning.
